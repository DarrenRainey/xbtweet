Welcome and thank you for using xbTweet! 

beta version 0.900 released.
This new version of xbTweet as many new features and updates:
- Receive notifications while watching movies or playing music
  You can specify when and how you wish to be notified about in the script's settings page.
- Better bit.ly links, let your followers to read more about what you're playing
  When activated in the script's setting page, xbTweet will include a short link in your tweets that will take your followers to imdb/last.fm to read more about the content.
